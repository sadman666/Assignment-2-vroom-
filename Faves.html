FAVOURITES

Why care?

Someone might ask, "what is the best car today?". To answer that, it might look easy firstly. But when you actually start to answer that, one hangs under a bridge of classes to prioritise.

Classes? Yes, there are road cars, off-road cars, SUVs, Trucks, Convertibles, sedans and so many if you categorise them according to tiny features they vary so much in.

"Whats the best in each class, then?" you might ask. well, the question is still hard, almost every manufacturers make unique-ly superior cars to survive the market competition. Its actually more dependent on what the buyer is looking for and what purpose shall the car be serving or where it will be mostly going

Since "best car" is going to create controversary, I am going to state my personal choices based on SPEED since thats my type. Hence, the name of this page- "FAVOURITES"
