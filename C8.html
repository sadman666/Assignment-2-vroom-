The Chevrolet Corvette C8

What are you looking at?

You are looking at a piece of art that has a memorable history surrounding its previous generations.

The previous generations of the corvette were all front-engined. This time it is mid-engined. Making it an amazing track monster by making the weight distribution more balanced.

This 495 horsepower monstrosity is powered by a 6.2l LT2 V8 and does 0-60MPH in 2.9s

All this becomes more astonishing for the 60,000$ pricetag on it. For a mid-engined covette that is capable of 0-60 under 3 seconds right out of factory, it can be considered as a win win.
