FAST? FASTER?

So, in 1886, Karl Benz officially gave birth to modern Cars by coming up with a patent called "Benz Patent Motorwagen"

Coming up with a wheel+cart combination that went upto 16 kmph was not enough. This figure might not sound much, but without functional brakes, it was DANGEROUS.

A safe car that went 16km/h was not enough for humanity. People needed cars that can go to places faster than a horse and carry more loads than a donkey much more efficiently.

After years of technological advancements, todays cars serve multiple purposes from supercars reaching upto 310 miles per hour to trucks than can carry 20 supercars on its trailers.
